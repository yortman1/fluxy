🍃 FLUXY The LI-6800 Time-Series Visualizer

A lightweight R Shiny app to trim and export stable sections of LI-COR LI-6800 logs for downstream analysis.

Download FLUXY

This is an academic/open-source tool, not a commercial product.

🧪 How to Run FLUXY

  1. Open R (or RStudio) and run:
    install.packages("shiny")
  2. Save app.R somewhere and open it in RStudio. (don't rename it!)
  3. Click "Run App" — that's it! 🚀

✨ Features you'll love

  • Load raw LI-COR data logs
  • Visualize multiple variables at once
  • Adjust axes and ignore outliers
  • Label segments in multiple categories
  • Automatically detect matching events and exclude artifacts
  • Export categories in a new column
  • Export to CSV, RData, or to your R environment!
